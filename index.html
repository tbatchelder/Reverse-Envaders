Reverse Envaders

So, what is this?

This is an old idea I had from 2013 that I would like to resurrent as an example of my ability.

So, back then, I was using Director. This was a great program from Macromedia (along with Flash). It allowed you to build entire programs using it's Lingo code. It even eventually had some of the first 3D modeling that you could get on the web. Sadly, Adobe bought it and basically killed it. Project dead.

Now that my site is closing, I need a new career. So I've been looking at my old ideas and seeing if they can be resurrected in some manner. This seems like a "simple" possibility.

Premise

It's the opposite of Space Invaders. Instead of controlling the cannon and shooting the aliens, YOU'RE the "aliens" and your mission is to destroy the cities. ... wow, that sounds a bit evil ... so, let's make a bit of a change. You're not an alien, you're a agent of Gia and your mission is to stop the advance of humans that are destroying the world. So, you're not an "Invader", you're an Environmental Invader.
